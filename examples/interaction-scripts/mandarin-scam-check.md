# 示例交互：诈骗检查（中文 / Mandarin)

**场景:** 王美玲（68岁）接到可疑电话，想检查是否是诈骗。

---

## 通话流程

**系统:** *电话接通*

**助手:** "您好！我在这里帮助您。今天您需要什么？"

**王美玲:** "我刚才收到一个电话，说是银行的，但是我不太确定..."

**助手:** "我明白了。让我帮您。我会检查这个号码是否安全。请告诉我电话号码。"

**王美玲:** "号码是... 1... 8... 0... 0... 5... 5... 5... 1... 1... 1... 1"

**助手:** "正在检查这个号码。请稍等。"

*系统查询Sync.me数据库*

**助手:** "小心！这个号码有547个诈骗举报。我建议不要接。您要我屏蔽这个号码吗？"

**王美玲:** "是的！请帮我屏蔽。"

**助手:** "为了您的安全，我已屏蔽这个号码。您很安全。"

**王美玲:** "谢谢！"

**助手:** "还有什么我能帮您的吗？"

**王美玲:** "现在几点了？"

**助手:** "现在是下午3点45分，星期天，10月19日。"

**王美玲:** "好的，谢谢。"

**助手:** "之后您还想做什么吗？"

**王美玲:** "不用了，谢谢。"

**助手:** "感谢您的来电。保重！"

## English Translation

**System:** *Call connects*

**Assistant:** "Hello! I am here to help you. What do you need today?"

**Wang Meiling:** "I just received a call claiming to be from the bank, but I'm not sure..."

**Assistant:** "I understand. Let me help you. I will check if this number is safe. Please tell me the phone number."

**Wang Meiling:** "The number is... 1... 8... 0... 0... 5... 5... 5... 1... 1... 1... 1"

**Assistant:** "Checking the number now. One moment."

*System queries Sync.me database*

**Assistant:** "Be careful! This number has 547 scam reports. I recommend not answering. Do you want me to block this number?"

**Wang Meiling:** "Yes! Please block it for me."

**Assistant:** "For your safety, I have blocked this number. You are safe."

**Wang Meiling:** "Thank you!"

**Assistant:** "Is there anything else I can help you with?"

**Wang Meiling:** "What time is it?"

**Assistant:** "It is 3:45 PM, Sunday, October 19th."

**Wang Meiling:** "Okay, thank you."

**Assistant:** "Do you want to do something after this?"

**Wang Meiling:** "No, thank you."

**Assistant:** "Thank you for calling. Take care!"
